## Welcome to ACV Datasets
Welcome to our **ACV Data Platform**! We provide a variety of datasets, including images, videos, and medical images. Below is a brief overview of these dataset categories and their locations.

## Datasets Overview

1. **Images Datasets** - Our image datasets cover various topics, ranging from object recognition to scene understanding. They are ideal for training and evaluating computer vision models.

2. **Videos Datasets** - Featuring a wide range of subjects, our video datasets are perfect for training video analysis and action recognition models.

3. **Medical Images Datasets** - We offer medical datasets containing high-quality images, such as CT scans, MRIs, and X-rays, suitable for medical image processing and analysis tasks.

We hope you find these datasets helpful for your research and projects!
